Sustainable Dev Schemes

Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme

The Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme (DEDS) is an initiative by the Government of India aimed at promoting entrepreneurship and fostering the growth of the dairy sector in the country. Launched under the Ministry of Animal Husbandry, Dairying, and Fisheries, the scheme provides financial assistance and support to individuals, groups, and organizations involved in the dairy business. Generates self-employment opportunities in the dairy sector, covering activities such as enhancement of milk production, etc.

Gramin Bhandaran Yojana

The Gramin Bhandaran Yojana is a scheme initiated by the Government of India to provide financial assistance for the construction of rural godowns (warehouses) and cold storage facilities. The primary objective of the scheme is to reduce post-harvest losses of agricultural produce, particularly perishable commodities, by enhancing storage infrastructure in rural areas. Scheme for the construction or renovation of rural warehouses or godowns for storing farm produce.

Sansad Adarsh Gram Yojana

The Sansad Adarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY) is a rural development initiative launched by the Government of India with the aim of transforming villages into model villages that showcase holistic and sustainable development. The scheme was launched by Prime Minister Narendra Modi on October 11, 2014, with the vision of Members of Parliament (MPs) adopting and developing one village each as a model village by 2019. Development of socio-economic and physical infrastructure of three villages each by 2019, and a total of eight villages each by 2024.

Rainfed Area Development Programme (RADP)

The Rainfed Area Development Programme (RADP) is a scheme initiated by the Government of India to address the developmental needs of rainfed areas, where agriculture largely depends on rainfall rather than irrigation. Launched by the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, RADP aims to enhance agricultural productivity, improve livelihoods, and reduce the vulnerability of farmers in rainfed regions.

National Scheme on Welfare of Fishermen

The National Scheme on Welfare of Fishermen is a central government initiative launched to address the socio-economic needs of fishermen and fishing communities across India. It aims to improve the living standards, health, and well-being of fishermen and their families, as well as to promote sustainable fisheries practices and provide support for their livelihoods.
